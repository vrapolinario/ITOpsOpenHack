# 🛡 Challenge 4: Policy, Compliance, and Data Loss Prevention

**Welcome to the final challenge.**
**Your mission:** bring AI workloads into compliance with enterprise governance standards.
Compliance teams have identified gaps across identity, networking, and configuration. Manual fixes aren’t scalable — policy enforcement is required.

⏱ Duration: ~45 minutes
🎯 Goal: Enforce governance and compliance without manual intervention

## 🧠 Scenario

AI services were deployed quickly to meet business demand. Governance was… an afterthought.
Now compliance teams are asking:

- Are these services configured correctly?
- How do we prevent future drift?
- How do we enforce standards consistently?

## 🚀 The Challenge

By the end of this challenge, you will have:

- Applied Azure Policy to AI services
- Enforced required configuration standards
- Identified non‑compliant resources
- Remediated issues using policy‑driven controls

## ✅ Success Criteria

You know you’re done when:

- Non‑compliant resources are clearly identified
- Policies prevent risky configurations
- Governance is automated, not manual
- AI workloads meet enterprise compliance expectations

## 🧭 Think Like an Operator

Ask yourself:

- How do we prevent this from happening again?
- Can governance scale as AI adoption grows?
- Would an auditor be satisfied with this setup?
