# 📊 Challenge 3: Monitoring, Logging, and Troubleshooting AI Workloads

**Welcome to Challenge 3.**
**Your mission:** gain operational visibility into AI workloads and prove you can troubleshoot failures. The business is reporting intermittent issues — but right now, IT/Ops has no clear insight into what’s going wrong.

⏱ Duration: ~45 minutes
🎯 Goal: Establish observability and troubleshooting capability for AI services

## 🧠 Scenario

AI-powered applications are failing sporadically:

- Requests time out
- Responses are throttled
- Authentication errors appear unexpectedly

Without proper logs and metrics, IT/Ops is flying blind.

## 🚀 The Challenge

By the end of this challenge, you will have:

- Enabled diagnostics and logging for AI services
- Integrated AI workloads into Azure Monitor and Log Analytics
- Identified common failure modes
- Demonstrated the ability to investigate and explain issues

## ✅ Success Criteria

You know you’re done when:

- You can trace a failing request end‑to‑end
- Logs clearly explain why something failed
- Metrics provide insight into service behavior
- Troubleshooting no longer relies on guesswork

## 🧭 Think Like an Operator

Ask yourself:

- How would I debug this at 2 a.m.?
- What signal tells me something is wrong?
- Can I explain this failure to a stakeholder?
